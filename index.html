What a Camera
Cannot See

Essay  ·  February 16, 2026  ·  1 min read

Is a thousand words enough?

The world is freaking out about images that do not represent reality. But Plato warned us 2,400 years ago not to trust shadows on a cave wall. A photograph of a mountain does not contain what the eye truly saw. The northern lights never look right in print.

Finding the truth may require going beyond the image — to see light not rendered in pixels, brush strokes, or shadows.
